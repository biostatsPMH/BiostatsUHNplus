To Setup SourceTree:

Select Options from the Tools Menu

Click on the Authentication tab at the top of the screen

Click Add under Accounts

Choose GitHub for the Hosting Service

Leave Preferred Protocol as HTTPs

For Credentials:

Change Authentication to Basic

This will allow you to enter your username (or email)

Then change Authentication back to OAuth 

Click Refresh Password, this will take you to a gitHub login

Login, click OK, and maybe OK again to go back to SourceTree

Now you should see your gitHub account under Accounts, click OK

Select Remotes from the top of the screen and all the repositories you have permission to access will appear, you can select one and click Clone to download onto your machine.
